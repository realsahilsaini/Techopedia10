FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS.

What is Techopedia?
Techopedia is the annual technical fest of IEEE SIES GST. This year we are celebrating its tenth edition.
What does the theme 'Epoch' imply?
To celebrate the glory that Techopedia has achieved over a decade we derived that nothing is grander than the concept of time. For Techopedia 10.0 we are looking to cruise through time and create a perceptive experience for the senses. Maneuvering through the past over the present and to the future is our endeavour.
What are benefits of participating in the events?
By simply participating you increase the likelihood of winning exciting cash prizes, coupons, goodies and national-level certificates.
What is the registration fee?
There is absolutely no registration fee. Your entry is absolutely free!
How will I stay up-to-date with the event timeline?

Is On-day registration open?
On-day registrations are unavailable! So register before the closing date for incredible fun through Techopedia X.
